**Section Header:** // Section 02: How It Works

**Main Headline:**

### Manifesto (4 principles):

**Line 1:**
> We put ambitious builders in the same room as clinical challenges that actually matter. When a clinician depends on what you build, you stop theorizing and start shipping.

**Line 2:**

**Line 3:**

### // Core Specs

**Spec 01: 8 Weeks**
> Not 6 months. Not a semester. 8 intense weeks to build, test, iterate, and sell. If you can't ship something meaningful in 8 weeks, you won't ship it in 8 months.

**Spec 02: Student-Run**
> Built by students, for students. No corporate sponsors dictating direction. No professors grading deliverables. Just founders helping founders build real companies.

**Spec 03: Real Problems**
> Sourced directly from hospitals, clinics, and healthcare organizations. Not "wouldn't it be cool if" ideas. Actual operational problems with real budgets behind them.

**Spec 04: Your Startup**
> You build it. You own it. 100% of the equity. We're not taking a cut. We're not "partnering." This is your company.

### FAQ Questions & Answers:

**Q1: Do I need an idea to apply?**
> **No.** The challenges come directly from hospitals, clinicians, researchers, and health-tech partners. We've already scoped the real problems. You bring the skills.

**Q2: Is this paid?**
> Not in salary. But you'll work on **real healthcare challenges**, build a working MVP, get hands-on mentorship, and gain visibility in the health innovation ecosystem. If you're serious, this can open real doors — including your **first paying customer**.

**Q3: Do we have to give up equity?**
> **No.** Your product, your pitch, your decision. **Whoever builds, owns.**

**Q4: Do I need MedTech experience?**
> **No.** We provide the domain access, the clinical partners, and the problem context. You just need the skills and the drive to solve hard problems.

**Q5: Do I have to speak German?**
> It helps on the business and sales side. If you're focused on tech or design, **English is fine**.

**Q6: Can I participate online?**
> **No.** We go out and touch grass. **In-person only, Munich.**

**Q7: Do I have to be a student?**
> Nope. Recent graduates and young professionals are welcome. No hard cut-off. If you're at the start of your career and want to build something real, **we want you in**.

**Q8: Can I do it next to studying?**
> Yes — but be realistic. If you're taking 6+ courses and a working student job, it might be a stretch. The **more time you put in, the more you get out**.

**Q9: Can I do it next to a working student job?**
> It depends. Some manage both, especially if they can adjust hours or workload. Just be **honest with yourself** about your capacity.
